Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 3, 2026

Important: We value your privacy. This policy explains how TripGenie collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our travel planning services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide a better service to all our users. This includes:

  • Personal Information: Email addresses and user profiles provided during authentication.
  • Trip Data: Travel dates, destination preferences, and itinerary details you generate.
  • Usage Data: Basic technical information collected automatically during app interaction.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the data we collect solely to provide, maintain, and improve TripGenie’s features, including AI-driven itinerary generation and cloud-based synchronization.

3. Third-Party Services

We integrate with best-in-class third-party services to power TripGenie:

  • Supabase: Used for secure authentication and database storage.
  • Google Maps & Places: Used for geographic location data and map visualizations.
  • OpenAI: Used for processing itinerary logic (data is processed anonymously).

4. Data Security & Retention

Your data is stored securely via Supabase using industry-standard encryption. We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the Service.

5. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.
